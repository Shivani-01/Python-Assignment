Expression: 
a=4*3/4+4/4+8-2+5/8	

Steps:
1)  Precedence of * and / are same and  left to right associativity is followed.
a=4*3/4+4/4+8-2+5/8
a=12/4+4/4+8-2+5/8

2) / has higher precedence than + and -
a=3.0+4/4+8-2+5/8
a=3.0+1.0+8-2+5/8
a=3.0+1.0+8-2+0.625

3) + and - have same precedence and left to right asociativity is followed
a=3.0+1.0+8-2+0.625
a=4.0+8-2+0.625
a=12.0-2+0.625
a=10.0+0.625
a=10.625

Answer:
a=10.625
